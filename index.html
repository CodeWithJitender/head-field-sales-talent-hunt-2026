Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Is there a registration fee? +
Registration details including any applicable fees will be shared upon signing up. Early bird participants may qualify for complimentary passes. Register now to stay updated.
Who can attend? +
Anyone passionate about sales — fresh graduates, experienced sales executives, BDMs, sales managers, and leaders. If you believe you can sell, you belong here.
What should I carry? +
Your registration confirmation, a valid photo ID, printed or digital copies of your CV/resume, and your confidence. Business formals are recommended.
Will interviews happen on-site? +
Absolutely. 50+ companies will conduct live interviews at dedicated Interview Lounges on Day 2. Spot offers will be extended to top candidates on the day itself.
Can freshers apply? +
Yes! Fresh graduates are one of our key audiences. Many companies are specifically looking to hire entry-level sales talent. No prior experience is needed — just drive and enthusiasm.
Will certificates be provided? +
Yes. All participants will receive a participation certificate. Top performers and award winners will receive special recognition certificates from Head Field.
How should I dress? +
Business formal or smart business casual is strongly recommended. You'll be meeting recruiters and interviewing live — dress to impress, dress to succeed.
